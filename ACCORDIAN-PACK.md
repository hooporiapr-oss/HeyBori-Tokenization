# LEVEL 1 — AWARENESS

## Free Episodes 001–010

Level 1 teaches the player how to see clearly. The goal is not to impress Bori. The goal is honesty. Every episode should push the player to recognize what is really happening inside their game, habits, identity, and choices.

---

## Episode 001 — Value

**Locked question:** What do you bring to the game when your shot is not falling?
**Spanish:** ¿Qué aportas al juego cuando tu tiro no está entrando?

**Angle:** A player’s value cannot depend only on scoring. If the shot is off, the player still has defense, communication, energy, decision-making, spacing, rebounding, leadership, and effort. The first awareness test is whether the player knows their value beyond points.

**Accordion heading:**
EN: Points Are Not Your Whole Value. <span>Your Game Has More To Say.</span>
ES: Los Puntos No Son Todo Tu Valor. <span>Tu Juego Tiene Más Que Decir.</span>

**Bori opener:**
EN: Think about your last game or practice. If your shot was not falling, what else did you bring? Be specific.
ES: Piensa en tu último juego o práctica. Si tu tiro no estaba entrando, ¿qué más aportaste? Sé específico.

**Dropdown choices:**

* I defended hard.
* I communicated and helped teammates.
* I kept my energy up.
* I disappeared when I was not scoring.
* I am not sure what I bring yet.

**Bori response logic:**

* Defense: affirm value beyond scoring; ask how they can make that reliable.
* Communication: affirm leadership; ask who benefited from their voice.
* Energy: affirm effort; ask how to bring it without needing points.
* Disappeared: affirm honesty; ask what one non-scoring action they can commit to.
* Not sure: affirm awareness; ask them to pick one area to test next game.

## Episode 002 — Social Media

**Locked question:** Are you building your game or just your image?
**Spanish:** ¿Estás construyendo tu juego o solo tu imagen?

**Angle:** Social media can showcase work, but it can also replace work. Posting is not progress. Highlights are not habits. The player must learn the difference between building a brand and pretending to be one.

**Accordion heading:**
EN: Posting Work Is Not The Same As Doing Work. <span>Know The Difference.</span>
ES: Publicar Trabajo No Es Lo Mismo Que Hacer Trabajo. <span>Conoce La Diferencia.</span>

**Bori opener:**
EN: Last time you posted or wanted to post basketball content, what were you trying to prove?
ES: La última vez que publicaste o querías publicar contenido de baloncesto, ¿qué estabas tratando de probar?

**Dropdown choices:**

* I wanted to show real progress.
* I wanted attention.
* I wanted coaches or people to notice me.
* I posted the good part but hid the struggle.
* I do not post much.

**Bori response logic:**

* Real progress: affirm; ask what improved off-camera.
* Attention: affirm honesty; ask what attention cannot fix.
* Coaches: redirect to substance; ask what a coach would see beyond clips.
* Hid struggle: affirm; ask what struggle would actually build trust.
* Do not post: note that silence is not automatically discipline; ask what they are building privately.

# Accordion HTML Generator Rules

For every episode file:

* Use the same CSS and JavaScript foundation.
* Change only the episode config object.
* Avoid backend.
* Avoid open chat.
* Use dropdown responses only.
* Optional short reflection input may exist, but it should not control unlock.
* Unlock next episode only when:

  1. All accordion sections are marked complete.
  2. Final dropdown answer is selected.
  3. Bori response appears.

Recommended completion data stored locally:

```js
localStorage.setItem('grh_episode_009_complete', 'true');
```

Recommended lock check:

```js
const previousComplete = localStorage.getItem('grh_episode_008_complete') === 'true';
```

For paid levels, use the payment/unlock page instead of relying only on localStorage.
